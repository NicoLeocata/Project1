Personal Notes

Reflection

The tool that inspires me the most is TailwindCSS. Its utility-first approach allows me to rapidly prototype designs without writing custom CSS. However, I noticed a trade-off: while it speeds up development, it can make the HTML less semantic and harder to read.

Another trade-off I observed is between creativity and convention. Tools like Bootstrap and TailwindCSS provide predefined styles and components, which can limit creativity but ensure consistency and faster development.

For my portfolio project, I would use TailwindCSS to quickly create a responsive layout and customize it for a unique look. I would also consider using Animate.css for subtle animations to enhance user experience without overwhelming the design.
